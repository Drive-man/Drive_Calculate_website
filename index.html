An "About Us" page is a section on a website that builds trust by introducing a company's mission, values, history, and team. It is often the second most-visited page on a site and helps visitors connect with the brand's story and purpose, ultimately influencing their decision to buy. Key elements include telling the brand's story, showcasing the people behind the business, and clearly explaining how the company can solve a customer's problem.
